About Us

SERVED EVERY DAY SINCE 2024

Code Crackers opened on 25 July 2024. Head Chef/Owner Angus Henderson began making Chinese cuisine and orchestrating a takeaway service out of his humble home in Stirling. Today, Code Crackers has a permanent location in Glasgow, Scotland. Beloved by many, the restaurant celebrates 20+ hours of classic, made from scratch Scottish cooking.
